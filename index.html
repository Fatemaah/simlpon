Every beauty lover remembers the moment they fell in love with makeup. For some it’s a memory of their mother, for others it was their rebellious youth. Makeup is, and will always be expression, art, and symbolic of a moment in time..


As we continue to craft outside beauty, don’t forget the life long journey towards inner beauty and self-acceptance. They can and should co-exist.
